# Privacy Policy for IZRK Intranet

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the IZRK Intranet application, including its web and mobile versions, published under the name `IZRK Intranet`.

## Controller and contact

IZRK Intranet is operated for internal organizational use by the Institute of Slovenian Ethnology at ZRC SAZU (`IZRK`, `ZRC SAZU`).

For privacy questions, requests, or complaints, contact:

- `izrk@zrc-sazu.si`

## Scope

This policy explains what information the app processes, why it is processed, how long it may be kept, and what choices users have.

The app is intended for authorized internal users. It is not directed to children.

## Information we collect

Depending on how the app is used, the app and related backend services may process the following categories of data:

- Account and identity data: name, surname, e-mail address, user role, password reset details, and authentication/session information.
- Contact data: work phone number and mobile number where provided by the organization or by the user.
- Profile and preference data: language preference, theme preference, and profile changes made inside the app.
- Reservation data: calendar names, reservation titles, reservation details, locations, dates, times, all-day flags, reservation authors, and related subscription feed URLs.
- Messaging data: recipient groups, recipient contact details, message subjects, e-mail bodies, SMS content, CC entries, sender information, and send history.
- Bulletin content and revisions: content published in the `Aktualno` section and its revision history.
- Administrative and audit data: user management changes, reservation changes, calendar changes, messaging actions, and audit log entries associated with user actions.
- Technical and security data: server logs and diagnostic records that may include IP address, device/browser metadata, timestamps, request details, and error information needed to operate and secure the service.
- Local device storage data: the app stores a JWT session token, language setting, and theme setting in local storage on the device or browser.

## How we use information

We use personal data only as needed to provide and administer the app, including to:

- authenticate users and maintain signed-in sessions;
- show and update user profiles;
- create, display, edit, and export reservations and calendar subscriptions;
- create and send internal e-mail or SMS notifications;
- maintain bulletin board content and revision history;
- administer users, permissions, and access;
- create audit trails, investigate misuse, and protect the security of the service;
- maintain and improve app stability, including delivery of app updates on supported mobile builds.

We do not use personal data from this app for third-party advertising.

## Legal basis

Where applicable, processing is based on the organization’s legitimate interests in operating an internal business system, managing staff communication and scheduling, maintaining security, and complying with legal obligations. Some profile data may also be processed to perform organizational or contractual obligations.

## Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share data only in the following limited situations:

- with service providers that host, deliver, or support the app and its infrastructure;
- with communications providers used to send e-mail or SMS messages initiated through the app;
- with update-delivery infrastructure used for mobile app bundle updates;
- when required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

These recipients may process data only to provide services on our behalf or as required by law.

## International transfers

Some service providers or infrastructure components may process data outside the user’s country. Where this happens, appropriate contractual or organizational safeguards should be used.

## Data retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including operational, security, audit, backup, and legal reasons.

Retention periods may differ by data type. For example:

- session and device-side local storage data remain until the user signs out, the app clears them, or the user deletes app/browser data;
- profile, reservation, bulletin, messaging, and audit records may be retained while the account or organizational records remain active and for a reasonable period afterward for backup, accountability, and compliance purposes;
- server logs and diagnostics may be retained for security and troubleshooting for a limited period.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed through the app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## Mobile app behavior

The mobile app does not request access to precise location, contacts, camera, microphone, or photo library based on the current codebase.

The app requires network access to connect to the IZRK backend and may download application updates for supported mobile builds.

## Your choices and rights

Depending on applicable law, users may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or data portability for personal data processed through the app.

Users may also:

- update some account details directly inside the app;
- sign out and remove locally stored session data by clearing app/browser storage;
- contact the organization using the address listed above for privacy-related requests.

We may need to verify identity and organizational authorization before fulfilling a request.

## Third-party services

The app may rely on third-party infrastructure or libraries to deliver core functionality, including backend hosting, e-mail delivery, SMS delivery, and mobile app update delivery. Their processing is limited to supporting the service and is subject to their own terms and privacy practices where applicable.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version should be made available from the app listing, website, or another distribution channel used for IZRK Intranet.
